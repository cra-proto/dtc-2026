CRA's review

After we receive your DTC application, we review it to determine if you are eligible.

On this page

  • Check the status of your application
  • How the review process works

Check the status of your application

Check the status of your application using the progress tracker in your CRA account.You can find the progress tracker on the Overview page of both My Account and My Business Account.

Sign in to your CRA account

Status explanations

Received
Your disability tax credit submission has been received by the CRA.
In progress
Your submission is currently being reviewed.
Information requested
We have revised the target completion date because we need more information to complete our review.
Target completion date update
We need extra time to finish processing your application. Unless you are contacted by letter by the CRA, you do not need to do anything.
Completed
We have completed our review of your submission. We have mailed the results of our review to the person with the disability. If your application has been completed, you can check your DTC eligibility status in your CRA account, on the Benefits and Credits page under the Disability tax credit (DTC).
Tracking stopped
Your submission will no longer be tracked in the progress tracker, but we will still review what you sent us. If you have questions, you can get support with an issue.

If your application has been completed, you can check your DTC eligibility status in your CRA account. The information will be on the Benefits and Credits page under the Disability tax credit (DTC).

How the review process works

We review applications based on the information provided by the medical practitioner.

  1. [We receive your application]
  2. [We do the initial review, comparing the details in your application with the DTC eligibility criteria]
  3. In some cases, we may send the medical practitioner a letter asking for more information, such as documents to support your application

    If you are a medical practitioner with questions, refer to: Information for medical practitioners

  4. [If we asked for additional information, we review that information]
  5. When our review is complete, we send you a letter (notice of determination) that tells you CRA's decision.

If you send your application with your tax return, we will review your application before assessing your income tax return.

How long it takes

We try to process your application and send you a notice of determination as quickly as possible. It may take longer if information is missing.
